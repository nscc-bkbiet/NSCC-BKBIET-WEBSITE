About NSCC BKBIET

The Newton School Coding Club at BKBIET is an active community for tech and programming enthusiasts. The club is committed to the spread of knowledge and fostering a love for coding and technology among students. One of the major activities of the club is organizing coding events. As part of these events, students are challenged with problem-solving tests using various languages, thereby improving their understanding and skills in the chosen languages. The events also include a competitive aspect, with rankings being determined based on specific criteria.

Membership in the Newton School Coding Club is open to anyone with a desire to learn and grow in the field of coding. To become a member, prospective students need to complete a registration process, where they provide their name, email, phone number, branch, and year of study.
